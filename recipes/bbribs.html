CrockPot Baby Back Ribs!

A tasty and easy recipe

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 4 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds baby back ribs, trimmed
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (18 oz) bottle barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Season ribs with salt and pepper. Or use your own seasonings
  2. Pour 1/2 cup of water into the slow cooker and add the ribs. Scatter onion and garlic on top. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours or High for 4
  3. When the ribs are almost finished, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F(190 degrees C)
  4. Transfer ribs from the slow cooker to a baking sheet. Discard onion and garlic. Coat ribs with barbeque sauce.
  5. Bake ribs in oven until sauce starts to caramelize, 10 to 15 minutes
